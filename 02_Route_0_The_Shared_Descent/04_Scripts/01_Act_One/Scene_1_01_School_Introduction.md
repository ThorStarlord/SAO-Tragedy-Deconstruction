# Scene 1: School Introduction

## Summary
Makoto (Janus) is introduced as a shy, introverted classmate. Asuna notices his isolation and feels empathy due to her own strict upbringing. Kirito is present but focused on his own interests.

## Key Moments
- Makoto’s discomfort in crowds
- Asuna’s observation and empathy
- Kirito’s casual confidence

## Foreshadowing/Clues
- Makoto’s intense gaze when alone with Asuna
- Makoto mentions an interest in psychology books
- Asuna’s empathy for Makoto’s isolation

## Dialogue Snippet
> Asuna: "He always eats alone... Maybe he just needs someone to reach out."
